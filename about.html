I was born in Harrisonburg Virginia while my dad was in seminary at Eastern Mennonite Seminary. After finishing his degree my family of my mother father sister and I moved to Croghan NY where I have spent most of my life. Pictured here from left to right is me, my brother Samuel, sister Jessie, Father Ken and Mother Rosetta at our current house in Croghan NY.

One of my hobbies I have enjoyed for several years is road cycling. Since I started cycling I have biked in several bike rides/races and completed 5 centuries (100miles) with groups and by myself. My father got me into cycling and keeps my trying to stay faster than him, even though he is 31 years older than me.
